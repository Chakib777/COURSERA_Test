"Craving something rich, moist, and chocolatey? This easy Choco-Banana Cake combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with deep cocoa flavor for a delicious homemade treat. Perfect for any occasion, it's simple to make and guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth!"

......................................................................................

This Should be in Your shopping list:
  • 1 cup flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk

......................................................................................

This is How you Make the Cake: :)

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Mix mashed bananas, sugar, oil, and vanilla
  3. Add flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Stir in milk until smooth.
  5. Pour into a greased baking pan.
  6. Bake for 25-30 min. Let cool before serving.
